My Favorite Movies

Back to the Future Trilogy

I love time-travel movies and the Back to the Future Trilogy is one of my all-time favorite movies. I find the time-travel paradox so fascinating to think about and these movies use it in such a fun and imaginative way. It's the perfect mix of adventure and comedy.

Pirates of the Caribbean

Another adventure movie with a blend of comedy. Captain Jack Sparrow has got to be one of the coolest characters in movie history!
